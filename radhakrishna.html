About Us Krishna

Story Of RadhaKannaya

Radha's love was selfless and without any duality.She breathed for Krishna.Krishan's heart also yearns for Radha.
We cannot tell whose love was greatest.They loved both equally to each other.Radha and Krishna used to meet on the banks of river yamuna every evening with friends and spend their time by dancing,playing,music and singing.

Radha and Krishna forget time,family,friends and even the whole world when they were together.Such was their love for each other.One day Krishna tried to play a prank on Radha.He did not visit Radha for few days.Radha understood that it's all Krishna's leela but she missed Him so much that she got sick and weak day by day.

The very next day,Krishna came to see her.Radha was eager to see Krishna but she controlled herself.When Krishna tried to enter gopis blocked Him.Krishna become very upset and walked away with disappointment.when Radha came to know about it she also become sadder.

Then a lady who was passing by,saw the gopis in distress and asked about the problem.Gopis discussed whole incidence to that lady.They told her that Radha had become sick and weak.

That lady met with Radha and told her that by missing Krishna she had become weaker.Then Radha told that she really missed krishna.But Krishna didn't seem to miss her.Then lady held radha's hand firmly and Radha recognized the touch.it was the hands of Krishna.Krishna came out of disguise as lady and opened his hands wider and embraced Radha.
